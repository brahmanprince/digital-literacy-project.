Do's:

Do adjust privacy settings to control who sees your personal posts and information.

Do use professional headshots and accurate academic information on networking sites like LinkedIn.

Do think critically and verify sources before sharing news to avoid spreading misinformation.

Do engage constructively and respectfully in online discussions and forums.

Do keep your academic/professional profiles distinct from highly personal social media accounts.

Don'ts:

Don't post inflammatory, offensive, or discriminatory content.

Don't share sensitive personal information publicly, such as your exact location or financial details.

Don't engage with cyberbullies, internet trolls, or toxic online arguments.

Don't post compromising photos or text that could harm your future employment or internship prospects.

Don't plagiarize or share others' intellectual property without giving proper credit.
